# Bear Cafe — Design System

## Core Identity

Bear Cafe is a cozy illustrated social platform inspired by:
- cozy cafes
- honey and desserts
- warm night atmosphere
- soft pastel aesthetics
- relaxing game-like interfaces
- hand-drawn illustrations

The UI should feel:
- warm
- welcoming
- calm
- playful
- easy to understand
- soft on the eyes

Avoid corporate SaaS aesthetics.

---

# Visual Direction

STYLE KEYWORDS:
- cozy
- illustrated
- pastel
- warm
- soft shadows
- rounded
- cafe atmosphere
- social playground
- cute but readable
- game-inspired UI

Avoid:
- cyberpunk
- neon
- overly modern enterprise UI
- sharp edges
- harsh contrast
- visually noisy layouts

---

---

# Color Rules

- Prefer warm colors over cold colors
- Avoid pure black and pure white
- Avoid neon or highly saturated colors
- Reuse the same palette consistently
- Maintain soft visual harmony
- Avoid random accent colors

---

# Border Radius

Use soft rounded corners throughout the app.

Preferred:
- rounded-2xl
- rounded-3xl

Avoid:
- sharp corners
- square UI

---

# Shadows

Use soft shadows only.

Preferred:
- soft glow
- subtle depth
- layered shadows

Avoid:
- harsh shadows
- strong black shadows

---

# Spacing Rules

The UI should feel breathable.

Preferred spacing:
- p-4
- p-6
- gap-4
- gap-6
- space-y-4
- space-y-6

Avoid cramped layouts.

---

# Typography

The interface should prioritize readability.

STYLE:
- friendly
- casual
- human
- concise

Avoid:
- robotic AI wording
- overly formal Thai
- long paragraphs
- technical language for users

---

# Font Hierarchy

## Titles
Large, warm, visually clear.

Use:
- text-2xl
- text-3xl
- font-bold

---

## Section Titles

Use:
- text-lg
- text-xl
- font-semibold

---

## Body Text

Use:
- text-sm
- text-base

Avoid tiny unreadable text.

---

# UI Philosophy

Users should understand the page within 3 seconds.

Every major section must clearly communicate:
1. what it does
2. whether it can be used immediately
3. what action to take

---

# UX Rules

Prefer:
- large clickable cards
- obvious buttons
- visual hierarchy
- icons with labels
- low cognitive load

Avoid:
- too many choices at once
- dense dashboards
- long explanations
- hidden interactions

---

# Responsive Rules

The platform must work smoothly on:
- desktop
- tablet
- mobile

Always verify:
- banner scaling
- button wrapping
- overflow issues
- sidebar behavior
- text readability

Avoid horizontal scrolling.

---

# Component Rules

Inputs, dropdowns, modals, and buttons must remain visually consistent.

Reuse existing styles whenever possible.

Do not create entirely new visual styles unless necessary.

---

# Decorative Assets

Use illustrations and decorative assets for:
- mascots
- cafe items
- stickers
- leaves
- stars
- honey
- desserts
- hand-drawn accents

Decorations should support the UI, not overwhelm it.

---

# Illustration Style

Illustrations should feel:
- hand-drawn
- cozy
- warm
- slightly imperfect
- soft-lined

Avoid:
- hyper realism
- glossy 3D
- corporate vector art

---

# Motion & Animation

Use subtle animation only.

Preferred:
- fade
- float
- gentle hover
- soft transitions

Avoid:
- aggressive motion
- fast bouncing
- flashy effects

---

# Accessibility

Maintain readable contrast.

Buttons and cards should remain:
- easy to tap
- easy to read
- visually separated

Do not sacrifice usability for aesthetics.

---

# Product Identity

Bear Cafe is not a dashboard.

It is:
- a cozy social space
- a community lounge
- a relaxing online cafe
- a playful social platform

The experience should feel inviting and emotionally warm.
